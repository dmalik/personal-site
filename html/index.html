EXPERIENCE

Professional Career

I started my professional career at BlackBerry (then RIM) in 2004 as a Flash Developer. I spent 6 years developing learning material on how to use a BlackBerry and all of the software that BlackBerry created. In those 6 years I developed over 200 courses which allowed me to fine tune my skills in development, design, animation, video, 3D and most importantly user experience.

I was promoted to team lead in 2010 and managed 12 developers for just under 2 years. My team worked closely with the design team that featured specialists in 3D, Design and Video. Skills important to my success in this role were leadership, project management, communication, and mentorship.

In 2012 I moved on to become a Senior Application Development Consultant at BlackBerry where my primary focus was to help our top 100 partners create applications for the BlackBerry PlayBook and BlackBerry 10 OS in both Adobe AIR and HTML5. I worked with partners such as Bell Media, Evernote, and Kobo. I also wrote blog posts, managed bugs in JIRA, helped users on social media, and spoke on development at conferances such as FITC, GDC, and 360|Flex. I held that position for a little under 2 years.

Since October 2014 I have been working on a startup (beta coming fall 2015) and as of June 2015 started doing freelance work. Please contact me if you have a project in mind, I'd be happy to chat.
